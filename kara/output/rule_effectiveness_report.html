Executive Summary

This report evaluates the performance of all 15 AML detection rules currently deployed in the transaction monitoring platform. The analysis covers precision, recall, F1 score, alert volume, and true/false positive rates. The system currently covers an estimated 99.0% of transaction volume through at least one monitoring rule. The average system F1 score of 0.588 reflects a mixed performance profile, with three "Champion" rules demonstrating both high precision and recall, and four rules requiring immediate tuning attention due to excessive false positive rates.
